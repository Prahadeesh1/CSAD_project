Join our crew

Service Crew

We’re excited to welcome enthusiastic individuals to join our vibrant team! As a Service Crew member, you’ll be the friendly face of our cinema, ensuring customers receive exceptional service with speed and a smile. You’ll gain valuable experience in customer service and food handling while becoming part of a supportive, dynamic team that makes every day enjoyable.

What we're looking for:

  • A positive, approachable, and outgoing personality
  • Strong customer service skills
  • Flexibility to work various shifts, including weekends and holidays

Cleaning Crew

Join our team as a Cleaning Crew member and play a vital role in maintaining the cleanliness and hygiene of our cinema. You’ll ensure that all areas are spotless and welcoming, providing an exceptional environment for our guests to enjoy. If you take pride in keeping spaces tidy and are motivated to contribute to the overall experience, we’d love to have you on board.

What we're looking for:

  • A strong attention to detail and pride in maintaining cleanliness
  • Ability to work efficiently both independently and as part of a team
  • Ability to stand for long periods of time and perform physical tasks

Management

We are looking for dynamic and experienced individuals to join our Management Team. As a member of the management team, you’ll play a key role in overseeing the daily operations of our cinema, ensuring everything runs smoothly and efficiently. Your leadership and decision-making will help maintain a high standard of service and enhance the overall guest experience. If you have a passion for leadership and operational excellence, this is the perfect opportunity for you!

What we're looking for:

  • Proven experience in a managerial role, preferably in hospitality or customer service
  • Strong leadership and team-building skills
  • Excellent problem-solving abilities and decision-making skills
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks and work under pressure
