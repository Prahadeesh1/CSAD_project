Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if I lose my ticket or make a wrong booking?

Please proceed to the nearest PVC Cinema and seek assistance from our staff.

Can I refund or exchange my movie tickets?

Unfortunately, tickets are non-refundable and non-exchangeable. Please double-check your bookings before finalizing your purchase.

What are the payment methods for online booking?

We accept Mastercard, AMEX and Visa.

Can I hold an event or book a hall at PrismVerse Cinematics?

We welcome you to hold your events at PrismVerse Cinematics.
Our venue can accommodate various functions, including corporate meetings, weddings, private screenings and more.
Please write to us at booking@pvcinematics.com.sgwith your booking details and we will reply you within 3 working days.

Will my card details be stored in your system?

We do not store any card details in our system. Your card details are only used to be verified for payments.

Can my child watch a rated movie?

Children below the age of 16 are able to watch movies that are rated G, PG or PG13. Movies rated NC16, M18 or R21 are restricted to patrons of the minimum required age of 18. Identification Card (IC) may be requested by our staff for verification before admission.

Will my card be charged if I do not collect the ticket(s)?

Yes, as long as the transaction has been made, you will be charged.

Are there any student or senior discounts?

Yes, students with valid concession cards and seniors above 60 will enjoy ticket prices of $7.50, up from the standard price of $10.

What are your operating hours?

We operate daily from Monday to Sunday, 11am to 12pm.

What technologies are used in your cinemas?

At PVC Cinematics, we provide an exceptional cinematic experience with cutting-edge technology. Our cinemas are equipped with state-of-the-art sound systems, including high-definition speakers and powerful subwoofers, ensuring crystal-clear audio and immersive surround sound. Combined with our advanced projectors, every movie is brought to life with stunning visuals and rich, dynamic soundscapes, offering an unforgettable experience for all moviegoers."
